Opsta uplatnica

The basic payment method is via a General Payment Slip, which can be paid at a bank, post office, or exchange office ONLY IN SERBIA!

This method is also the slowest because it requires you to physically go somewhere to make the payment, and the payment needs to be verified by your bank and ours. The maximum time you will wait for us to receive your payment is until the following day by noon. The activation of services takes only a few seconds, but you have to wait for the funds to be credited to our account.

Paying with PayPal

You can pay via PayPal directly through this link. It is not necessary to create an account on the website, nor do you need a PayPal account to pay via card through PayPal. You can also make a payment from your PayPal account.



Paying by card or PayPal is the fastest option as the payment is immediately visible.



Paying with Skrill

You can make a payment via Skrill quickly and easily. Simply log in to your Skrill account, enter the amount and email address: contact@milanwebportal.com, and the payment will be visible immediately after the transaction is confirmed. You can make payments in various currencies.
