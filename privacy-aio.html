Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

All In One is a browser extension developed by Milan Web Portal. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that may be processed through our extension.

2. Data Collection

Our extension is designed with a "local-first" approach. This means:

  • We **do not** collect any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Your notes, web-tracking statistics, and settings are stored **locally** on your device using Chrome Storage.
  • We do not have access to your browsing history or passwords.

3. Third-Party Services

The "YouTube Dislike" feature uses the public Return YouTube Dislike API to fetch dislike counts. No personal data is sent to this service. The "Radio IN" feature streams audio directly from the official radio server.

4. Permissions Usage

Permissions like scripting and all_urls are used exclusively to provide core features such as Dark Mode, Ruler, and Enable Copy. These actions are performed locally in your browser.
